> **Slow-Cooker Black Bean Soup**
>
> **Ingredients:**
> - 1 lb dried black beans, soaked overnight
> - 1 large yellow onion, diced
> - 4 cloves garlic, minced
> - 2 carrots, diced
> - 1 red bell pepper, diced
> - 2 tsp ground cumin
> - 1 tsp smoked paprika
> - 1 bay leaf
> - 6 cups vegetable broth
> - Salt and pepper to taste
>
> **Instructions:**
> 1. Drain the soaked beans.
> 2. Add all ingredients to the slow cooker.
> 3. Cook on LOW for 8 hours or HIGH for 4 hours, until the beans are tender.
> 4. Remove the bay leaf. Optionally, blend half the soup for a thicker texture.
> 5. Season with salt and pepper, serve with lime wedges and cilantro.
