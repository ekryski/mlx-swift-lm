> **Acme Corp Travel Reimbursement Policy (2026 revision)**
>
> Employees may submit reimbursement requests for travel expenses incurred while conducting Acme business. All requests must be submitted within **30 days** of the trip's last day, accompanied by itemized receipts. Requests submitted after the 30-day window require written approval from the employee's department head and the Finance team.
>
> Reimbursable expenses include: airfare (economy class on flights under 6 hours, business class on flights over 6 hours), ground transportation (rideshare, taxi, rental car at the mid-size tier or below), lodging (up to $250/night in major metro areas, $150/night elsewhere), and meals (up to $75/day for the traveler).
>
> Non-reimbursable: alcohol, in-flight upgrades, personal entertainment, fees for flight changes due to non-business reasons, and any expenses for travel companions who are not Acme employees on Acme business.
